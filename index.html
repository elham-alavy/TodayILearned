DR. MAHMOUD HESSABI · محمود حسابی
23 Feb 1903 — 3 Sep 1992 · Tehran → Paris → Geneva
The father of modern physics in Iran. A man who studied at the Sorbonne, returned to Iran and built its science and engineering faculties from scratch. Nuclear physicist, senator, minister of education, and first CEO of the nationalized Iranian oil company. He believed science belongs to everyone — no borders, no walls. His house in Tehran is now a museum. A street bears his name. 🐰 khargosh thinks about him sometimes.
> "A nation that does not cultivate science will always be dependent on others." — Dr. Mahmoud Hessabi

// IN MEMORY · MUSIC · NOT HERE ANYMORE · FOREVER LOVED
JOE DASSIN · جو داسن 🎵
5 Nov 1938 — 20 Aug 1980 · Brooklyn → Paris → Tahiti 🌺
Born in Brooklyn, heart forever French. Joe Dassin sang in French, German, Spanish, Italian, Greek, and English. He sold 25 million records worldwide. He was American on paper but French at heart — his own words. Les Champs-Élysées, Et si tu n'existais pas, L'Été Indien — songs that don't age, don't fade, don't leave. He died of a heart attack in Tahiti at just 41 years old. Too soon. Way too soon. 🐰 khargosh still hums his songs on quiet evenings.
> ♪ "Aux Champs-Élysées... au soleil, sous la pluie, à midi ou à minuit — il y a tout ce que vous voulez." ♪

2024-03-07data-engineering
WHAT IS APACHE SPARK — AND WHY IT CHANGED EVERYTHING
(\(\ ( -.-) < "spark goes brrr 🚀" o_(")(") 🐰 khargosh says:
Apache Spark is a distributed data processing engine — it splits your data across many computers and processes them all at once. Before Spark, processing huge datasets took hours. Spark does it in minutes by keeping data in memory instead of writing to disk every step. For public health data — think millions of patient records — this is a game changer.
> "Today I learned that Spark doesn't just process data faster — it changes what questions you can even afford to ask."
(\(\ (^.^) o_(")(") 🟢
source: Apache Spark docs + hands-on practice
2024-11-19cloud
AZURE BLOB STORAGE — WHAT IT IS IN PLAIN ENGLISH
(\(\ (^.^) < "data in the cloud! ☁️" o_(")(") 🐰 discovers Azure
Azure Blob Storage is basically a giant hard drive in the cloud. "Blob" stands for Binary Large OBject — it stores any kind of file. For data pipelines, it's the landing zone where raw data arrives before being processed. Think of it as the inbox of your data architecture.
> "Today I learned that the cloud is just someone else's computer — but a very well-organized one."
(\(\ (^.^) o_(")(") 🟢
source: Microsoft Azure docs
